Ohalo

Ohalo II is an archaeological site located on the Sea of Galilee, It is one of the best-preserved hunter-gatherer archaeological sites dated to around 23,000 BP.

The site is significant for being the first evidence for the earliest small-scale plant cultivation, some 11,000 years before the onset of agriculture. Wikipedia
